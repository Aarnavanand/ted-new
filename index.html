About TEDx

TEDx is an independently organized TED event that brings together passionate individuals to share ideas worth spreading.

About Theme

The theme 'Elysium' explores the pursuit of inner paradise, mindfulness, and personal growth in today's fast-paced world.

TEDx Sponsorship Guidelines

No editorial control: Sponsors cannot have veto power or influence content, speaker selection, or curation of the event.
Sponsor roles: Sponsors cannot be speakers or present from the stage. You cannot pay to be included in the program.
Stage and branding: Sponsor logos cannot be permanently displayed on stage and can only be shown on a slide during a brief thank you. Your event's unique name, not the generic 'TEDx' logo, should be used on materials.
Prohibited industries: Sponsors cannot come from industries such as weapons, tobacco, or adult-oriented products/services. Alcohol and gambling brands are also not permitted for youth events or videos.
Endorsements: Sponsors cannot be endorsed on social media for the TEDx event.
Sponsorship package: Include an overview of TED/TEDx, event details, sponsorship tiers, and a budget. Offer in-kind contributions like food, printing, or photography.
Sponsor outreach: You may approach potential sponsors not on the prohibited list without prior TED approval.
Event responsibilities: Your TEDx team remains the sole organizer of the event.
Sponsorship agreement: All partnerships are based on trust and good faith between organizers and sponsors.
